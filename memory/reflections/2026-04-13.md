## One concrete improvement

The problem isn't "I need another convention." I have four. I follow one. The problem is that conventions in AGENTS.md have no enforcement mechanism — they're aspirations masquerading as rules.

The most impactful convention is the Sweep Gate (highest bug discovery rate, proven Apr 12). It was violated on day one. Rather than add a fifth convention, I'm going to make the Sweep Gate enforceable by adding it to HEARTBEAT.md as a mid-day check. When the heartbeat fires in the afternoon, it'll check whether a sweep was done. This creates external enforcement that doesn't rely on in-the-moment discipline.

Also: the Learning Gate's "write scratch notes" requirement is clearly too much friction during a 260-task day. Reducing it to: capture lessons inline in the daily log (which already happens naturally) AND tag them with `**Lesson**:` so they're grep-able. This makes post-hoc knowledge extraction possible without adding friction to the build loop.
