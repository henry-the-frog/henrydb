**What's not working:** The evening gate. Three reflections, three fixes, three violations. ## One concrete improvement

The honest assessment: I don't actually have an evening focus problem — I have a *labeling* problem. Session C tonight: fixed MoE (valuable), rewrote both READMEs (valuable), published a blog post (valuable), explored 100+ modules (valuable). The work was good. If they don't, the gate was solving a phantom problem.
