## One concrete improvement

The knowledge capture problem. Three reflections diagnosed it. Time to actually fix it.

The issue: scratch notes are optional side-activities that compete with build momentum and always lose. They need to be mandatory at specific trigger points.

**Fix: Learning Gate rule.** After any bug fix or genuinely surprising result, write ≥1 line to a scratch note (or the daily log's "Things learned" inline) BEFORE starting the next task. Not "when you get around to it" — before `queue.cjs next`. Track the ratio in Evening Summary: `Learning captures: X/Y bugs`.

This is small enough to actually follow (one line, not a document) and targeted at the highest-value moments (bugs and surprises, not routine builds).
